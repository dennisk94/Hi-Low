Instructions

Rules

Guess a secret number in as few tries as possible. After you input a number, press the guess button and you will be told whether it is higher or lower than the secret number. You can also press the help button to see how close your number is to the secret number

1 Player

Choose the difficulty, write your name and press play. The game will randomly generate a number within the number range that corresponds with the chosen difficulty level.

2 Player

Select the difficulty and write your names. One player will choose a number within the appropriate difficulty range and the other player will try to guess the secret number.

Difficulty Levels

Easy: 1-1024, Medium: 1-2048, Hard: 1-4096
